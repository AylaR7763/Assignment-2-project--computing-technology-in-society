ENHANCING ONLINE LEARNING THROUGH ADAPTABILITY

"Online learning materials should include activities for the different styles, so that learners can select appropriate activities based on

their preferred learning style." (Anderson, 2011, p. 27)

Personalized Learning Experiences:

Learning materials offer a spectrum of activities to cater to varying learning styles.

Learners have the freedom to choose activities that resonate with their preferred style.

Fostering Engagement:

Adaptability enhances engagement and participation. p>

When learners can select activities that align with their learning style, they become more engaged in the learning process.

Engaged learners are more likely to retain and apply knowledge effectively.
