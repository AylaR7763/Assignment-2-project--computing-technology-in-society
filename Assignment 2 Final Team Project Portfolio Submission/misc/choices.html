FLEXIBILITY AND EFFICIENCY IN ONLINE EDUCATION

“Because of the flexibility of online education, students may take for granted that their online coursework can easily fit into their typical

schedule.” (Globokar & Lynn, 2010)

Flexibility as a Key Feature:

Online education offers flexibility, allowing students to learn at their own pace.

Students often find it convenient to integrate online coursework into their daily routines.

This flexibility enhances accessibility and engagement in learning.

Efficiency of Online Learning:

Efficient time management is a hallmark of online education.

Students save time on commuting and can dedicate more time to coursework.

This efficient use of time benefits both students and educators.
