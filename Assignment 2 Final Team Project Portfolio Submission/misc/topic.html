IMPORTANCE OF ONLINE EDUCATION IN UNIVERSITIES.

OUR TOPIC

The learning world has been changing since the past decade, 10 years ago learning was confined to physical classrooms, and students

primarily relied on paper-based resources while attending lectures in person.. But in today's world we have computers for online

classes, online resources, and study guides.

WHY IS IT INTERESTING AND IMPORTANT

In today's world, online learning has emerged, redefining how we access knowledge and develop essential skills. This explores

the importance of online learning in our modern world. From its accessibility to its adaptability, we must look into why online learning has

become an essential part of contemporary education in universities.

ex. Martin, C. D., Huff, C., Gotterbarn, D., & Miller, K. (1996). A framework for implementing and teaching the social and ethical impact of computing. Education and Information Technologies, 1(2), 101-122.
